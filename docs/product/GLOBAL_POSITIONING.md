# Global Positioning

FluentOS is a global speaking-first language app that helps anyone become fluent through daily AI conversation, correction, repetition, and review — one active language at a time.

Short positioning: Global-first. Native-language-aware. Speaking-first.

Tagline: Speak one language fluently before you split your focus.

## Target Regions

FluentOS is built for US, India, Japan, Germany, Europe, Korea, Latin America, Middle East, and global learners.

## Global Language Strategy

- Keep base language and target language separate.
- Support learners from the language they think in, including Indian languages and global base languages.
- Keep target language options global, with support status marked as supported, preview, or coming soon.
- Localize missions, accents, examples, and pricing by region over time.

## India-Aware, Not India-Only

India remains a major supported region and Indian language support remains important. It is not the whole brand. FluentOS should speak naturally to Indian learners while also serving learners in the US, Japan, Germany, Europe, Korea, Latin America, the Middle East, and everywhere else.

## One Active Language Philosophy

Free learners focus on one active language at a time so missions, correction memory, repetition, review, and progress stay aligned. Pro can later unlock multiple active journeys after the core loop is proven.

## MVP Exclusions

The MVP has no map, social, dating, chat, voice rooms, nearby users, or meetups. FluentOS starts as a private speaking coach before any future social practice is considered.
